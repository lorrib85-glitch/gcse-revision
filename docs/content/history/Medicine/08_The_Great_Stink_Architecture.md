## Navigation spine (6 parts)

Every Medicine module must be built as six clear navigation parts. These titles should appear in the module journey/progress navigation so the student always knows where they are in the story.

1. **A City That Could Not Breathe** ŌĆö intro hook, germ theory/public health recall and roadmap.
2. **Cholera, Crowding and Filth** ŌĆö industrial towns, water, sewage and epidemic disease.
3. **Miasma Meets Evidence** ŌĆö Chadwick, Snow and the slow challenge to old explanations.
4. **The Government Finally Moves** ŌĆö Public Health Acts, local boards and Bazalgette's sewers.
5. **Prevention Becomes Public** ŌĆö significance, limits and change/continuity in public health.
6. **Exam Prep: Why Public Health Changed** ŌĆö examiner traps, factor judgement and exam practice.

## 4. Build recommendations

1. Keep the module as a prevention/public health story, not a treatment story.
2. Make Snow, Chadwick and Bazalgette distinct.
3. Do not imply Snow proved germ theory to everyone immediately.
4. Use Part 6 for factor-based judgement: government, individuals, science and technology.
