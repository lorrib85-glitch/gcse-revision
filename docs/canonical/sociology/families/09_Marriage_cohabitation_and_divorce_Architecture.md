## 6. Build notes

The trend graphs and cause chain give this episode a data-interpretation feel and connect well to research methods using official statistics.

Keep the episode balanced. Do not make divorce a simple decline story. The stronger sociological answer is that divorce and cohabitation can be interpreted differently: decline in commitment, increase in choice, escape from inequality, or evidence of changing expectations.

The declining birth rate and smaller average family sizes should be noted briefly as part of the broader picture of social change driving marriage/cohabitation trends, not treated as a separate topic.

For a 15-year-old audience, the cleanest hook is choice vs commitment: are people less serious about relationships, or less willing to stay in relationships that do not work?
