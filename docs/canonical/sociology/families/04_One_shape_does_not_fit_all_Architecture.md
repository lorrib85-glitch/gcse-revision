## 6. Build notes

Make diversity concrete, not abstract. Show how families differ because of culture, class, life stage, generation and organisation. Demographic trends (birth rate, death rate, ageing population, migration) should be woven in as explanations for why family forms have changed ‚Äî not treated as a separate topic.
